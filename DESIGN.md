# Catalyst Data Design Document

Catalyst Data is the shared evidence and measurement layer for Sustainable Catalyst.

It exists to answer seven practical questions:

1. What entity is being measured?
2. What indicator is being used?
3. What reporting period applies?
4. What value was recorded?
5. What source supports the value?
6. How confident is the record?
7. What method notes or review limits should travel with the output?

## Scope

Catalyst Data supports structured measurement, source discipline, provenance, and reviewable exports. It is designed to interoperate with Global Impact Catalyst, Catalyst Analytics R, Narrative Risk, Catalyst Canvas, and Sustainable Catalyst 1.0.

## Boundary

Catalyst Data structures evidence. It does not verify truth, certify compliance, or guarantee impact.
